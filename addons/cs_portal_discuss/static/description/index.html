Overview

The Portal Discuss, Chat & Meetings Hub module extends Odoo's collaboration capabilities to portal users, enabling seamless and secure communication directly within the Odoo portal.

  • Allow portal users to chat directly with internal users and specific individuals.
  • Enable participation in group discussions and public or private channels.
  • Send and receive messages in real time with full chat history visibility.
  • View and attend meetings using Odoo's built-in meeting and calendar features.
This module is ideal for organizations that want to centralize customer and partner communication inside Odoo, especially in regions where external meeting or messaging platforms are restricted or not permitted.

Once the module is installed, you can create or schedule meetings in Odoo and share the meeting link directly with clients or portal users, allowing them to join meetings and participate in discussions seamlessly through the Odoo portal.

03

Once a customer or portal user joins the meeting, any message shared during the meeting will instantly appear in the meeting chat. The same conversation is also synchronized and visible in the Discuss module within the Odoo backend for internal users.

04

Once a client or portal user logs in, they will see a dedicated Discussions section in the portal. From this section, users can view all their ongoing and past discussions, meeting conversations, shared messages, and related meeting records in one centralized place.

05

When a portal user clicks on any discussion or chat, they are redirected to that specific conversation where they can exchange messages and attachments without joining a meeting. Portal users can communicate in existing group or personal chats, but they cannot create new chats, channels, or invite additional members.
